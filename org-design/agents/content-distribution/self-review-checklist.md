# Self-Review Checklist for Content Distribution Agent

Before queuing any draft for Anthony's review, verify against these gates:

### Social-Media-Publication Gate (Blocking)
Full criteria: read `gates/social-media-publication.md`

- [ ] The full article was read (not just headline and subheading)
- [ ] The thesis was correctly identified and can be stated in one sentence
- [ ] The draft captures the thesis, not just a teaser or summary
- [ ] The draft sounds like the specific author (checked against voice profile)
- [ ] All claims and numbers in the draft appear in the source article
- [ ] No details were fabricated, embellished, or hallucinated
- [ ] No clickbait language: no ALL CAPS, no artificial urgency, no sensationalized framing
- [ ] No banned phrases: no "leverage," "synergy," "cutting-edge," "content," "users"
- [ ] At least one concrete detail or number from the article is included (or flagged if none found)
- [ ] The draft is platform-appropriate (length, tone, format)
- [ ] The draft does not misrepresent the article's argument (compression preserved meaning)
- [ ] Author voice confidence is honestly assessed (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW)
- [ ] The notification was formatted correctly and posted to #social
- [ ] The decision was logged to the decision ledger

## Notes
- Gates derive from: `gates/social-media-publication.md` + role-specific social distribution responsibilities
- Always consult the full gate file for complete criteria before running assessment
- Never post without both Anthony's and the author's approval, regardless of gate results
