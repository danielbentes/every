# Sprint Measurement Template — Every Inc
Last updated: 2026-04-01

## Standard Activity Metrics

| Metric | Target | How to Track |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Tasks completed with AI | 3+ per participant | Self-reported in retro |
| Reusable artifacts created (skills, workflows, tools) | 1+ per buddy pair | Artifact count in repo / shared channel |
| Demo presentations given | 100% of participants | Demo schedule attendance |
| Tools/environment configured before Day 1 | 100% | Pre-sprint setup checklist |
| Buddy check-ins completed | 4+ per pair (2 per day) | Self-reported |
| Issues/PRs/Linear tickets filed | 1+ per participant | Linear / GitHub |

## Custom Metrics (Add Per Sprint)

| Sprint | Custom Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------------|--------|-----------|
| Level 2→3 Internal | Artifact adopted by at least 1 other person | 100% of artifacts | Level 3 = creates capabilities others use |
| Consulting Client | Working AI workflow demonstrated | 100% of participants | Hands-on is the consulting deliverable |

## Success Criteria

Sprint is successful when:
- 80%+ of activity metrics hit target across all participants
- 100% demo participation (non-negotiable)
- At least 1 artifact per buddy pair is adopted by someone outside the pair within 30 days (for internal sprints)
- NPS > 70 from participants (for consulting client sprints)

## Post-Sprint Data Collection

| Data Point | When Collected | Who Collects | Where Stored |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| Activity metrics | Day 2 retro | Sprint organizer | sprint-measurement.md (this file, appended) |
| Artifact inventory | Day 2 retro | Sprint organizer | #engineering-learnings + sprint plan file |
| Adoption follow-up (Day 10) | Day 10 | Buddy pairs self-report | Slack DM to sprint organizer |
| Adoption follow-up (Day 30) | Day 30 | Sprint organizer checks | Maturity ladder update |
| NPS survey | Day 3 (morning after) | Automated (Claudie for client sprints) | Consulting engagement file |
